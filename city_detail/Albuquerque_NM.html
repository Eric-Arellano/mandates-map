Parking Reform Network

Albuquerque, NM

Citation 1

Summary

Albuquerque New Mexico has eliminated off street parking requirements in the downtown and SU-1 Transit Oriented Development. They have also reduced parking minimums in many other areas and had on street parking account for off street parking by different reduction rates in areas like transit oriented developments.
