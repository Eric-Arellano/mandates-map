Parking Reform Network

Traverse City, MI

Citation 1

Summary

Traverse City does not require parking for residential uses citywide. No car parking for any uses is required in the C-4 (Regional Center) District and the Grand Traverse Commons District. Also, properties located within 500 feet from the public transit center or parking decks have no parking minimums.
