Summary

In Fall 2020, the Santa Rosa City Council adopted a new Downtown Station Area Specific Plan that revised Downtown parking policy to eliminate minimum parking requirements within the Downtown Station Area boundary. This policy was codified and went into effect on January 1, 2021.
