Parking Reform Network

Eugene, OR

Citation 1

Summary

Eugene has eliminated parking minimums in the downtown and university districts. Parking requirements are reduced in various overlay districts.The maximum number of parking spaces for non-residential uses may not exceed 125 percent of the minimum spaces.
