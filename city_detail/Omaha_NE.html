Parking Reform Network

Omaha, NE

Summary

Minimum parking requirements have been eliminated for all land uses in the central business district (CBD) and neighborhood business districts (NBD). For TOD-1, there are no minimum parking requirements. For TOD-2, TOD-3, TOD-4 the minimum number of required parking spaces is 50 percent of the specified minimum requirements.
