Fruita, CO

Summary

The city has taken steps to eliminate parking minimums for reconstruction of existing buildings and reduce requirements for new construction in the DMU zone (downtown mixed-use zone). ADUs in that zone require no parking. Elsewhere, however, parking requirements remain fairly high.
