Miami, FL

Citation 1

Summary

Minimum requirements are waived for newly constructed buildings downtown. Certain small developments outside downtown are also exempt. Developers of new buildings near transit corridors under 10,000 sq ft can also be exempt. Large buildings near transit corridors can pay fees for exemptions.
