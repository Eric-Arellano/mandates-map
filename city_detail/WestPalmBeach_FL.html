West Palm Beach, FL

Citation 1

Summary

Reduced Parking minimums with Parking Maximums in CBD and special districts. No parking minimums in Okeechobee Business district with soft and hard parking maximums, if the developer wants to exceed the soft max they have to pay into the transit operations budget. No parking minimum for microunits in CBD.
