Parking Reform Network

South Burlington, VT

Summary

South Burlington has no minimum vehicle parking requirements city-wide, except in the case of requirements, which were reduced, for multi-family residential use (0.75-1.5 spaces per dwelling unit depending on number of bedrooms).
