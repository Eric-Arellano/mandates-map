Summary

The City of Penticton eliminated parking requirements for downtown commercial development in 2012 and has seen close to $100M in new development activity taking place. All of this, including removal of lanes of traffic downtown and turning them into wider sidewalks is creating a vibrant, walkable and sustainable downtown community.
