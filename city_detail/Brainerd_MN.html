Parking Reform Network

Brainerd, MN

Citation 1

Summary

Brainerd has no off street parking requirements for the following zones: B-4 (General Business) District, B-5 (Commercial Amusement) District, B-6 (Washington Street Commercial) District, I-1 (Light Industry) District, I-2 (General Industry) District. Also, there are exemptions for further reduced parking 515-22-9: Parking Deferment.
