Parking Reform Network

Tigard, OR

Donate to support this project!

Summary

No parking minimums in the Tigard Triangle, a 550-acre mixed-use zone slated for light rail service. Reduced or eliminated parking minimums for residential uses in all but the highest-density zone through transit-proximate reductions and on-street parking credits. No parking minimums in the Downtown Plan District Main Street Subarea. No parking minimums for regulated affordable housing in the Downtown Plan District.
