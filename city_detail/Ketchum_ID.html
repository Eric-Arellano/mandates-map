Ketchum, ID

Citation 1

Summary

In the community core and tourist zone, residential units under 750 square feet are exempt from providing any parking. Community housing, restaurants and food service, the first 5,500 sf of retail use, the first 5,500 sf of assembly use (theaters, conference centers etc.), are exempt from providing any parking in the community core and tourist zone.
